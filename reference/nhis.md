# National Health Interview Survey (NHIS) 2017

The National Health Interview Survey (NHIS) monitors the health of the
civilian non-institutionalized U.S. population through the collection
and analysis of data on a broad range of health topics.

## Format

a data frame with 26,742 observations of 1,414 variables.

## Source

https://archive.cdc.gov/www_cdc_gov/nchs/nhis/nhis_2017_data_release.htm

## Details

This dataset was used by Perez-Lebel (2022) in their paper Benchmarking
missing-values approaches for predictive models on health databases."
More information is available here:
https://github.com/aperezlebel/benchmark_mv_approaches
